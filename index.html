About Me

"Hello World!!!"

My Name is Dave Disney and I currently live in New Jersey. Originally, I was born in New York City and still consider myself a New Yorker at heart and NYC will always be my home.

I am a seasoned professional business owner with more than 20+ years of experience providing services to national as well as international businesses. In addition, I have extensive knowledge of selling and marketing business to business services to Senior Executives and C-Level Managers of Fortune 500 Companies.

Highly customer-centric with excellent relationship building skills as evidenced by achievement in increasing annual sales yearly, primarily through referrals, with an average of 90% customer retention.

Self-confident, organized and motivated. Bringing creativity and an entrepreneurial spirit to all business endeavors.

I have always been interested in computers and technology and currently enrolled at Rutgers University Coding Bootcamp with a concentration on Full Stack Web Development. Excited to explore this new career path and learn as much as I can. My expected graduation date is September 2017.

Accomplishments and Recognitions:

• On Air Narrator Volunteer, New Jersey State Library Talking Book and Braille Center (TBBC):
Narrator of reading service for the The Adult Services department of The New Jersey State Library Talking Book and Braille Center (TBBC). It serves New Jersey adult residents who have difficulty reading because of a physical impairment, a reading disability or a vision challenge. Broadcast on digital TV, on cable (Comcast & Verizon FIOS), and live on the internet.

• Past Member/Chairman, Township Environmental Commission.
Appointment by Mayor of Township to serve a three (3) year term. Also, appointed as the Township Environmental Commission Liaison to the New Jersey Highlands Coalition.

• Past Member, Hunterdon County Mental Health Board:
Oversight of matters related specifically to Mental Health Services in the County. Assist in the development of the County’s Mental Health Plan, assist in the recommendation of State funding from the Division of Mental Health Services of the New Jersey Department of Human Services with whom it has direct linkage and helps evaluates effectiveness of the State funded programs.

• Past “On Air” Co-Host and Producer for the Mental Health Matters Radio Show. The show was awarded an Ambassador Award from the New Jersey Governor’s Council for Excellence in Media Coverage.

•Civilian Service Awarded by the Willingboro F.O.P. Lodge 38

•Honorary Membership received from the Fraternal Order of Police Lodge 38

•The Citizen's Campaign- Citizen Legislator and Certified Citizen Journalist.
